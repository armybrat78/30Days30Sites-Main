About me

Introduction

I graduated in B.E (Electrical and Electronics Engineering) in 2015. I currently work as a Research Associate at Samskrti Foundation, a research organisation based in Mysore, Karnataka, India. I am part of several projects that are soley dedicated to knowledge mining from old Indian manuscripts and texts and representing them in a better way, suitable for the modern world. I initially started self-learning web development in order to help in the projects at my workplace.

I started my learning journey at Free Code Camp, a community-led coding camp that provides free learning paths and certifications. I am an active cohort-member of Chingu Cohorts which is a a global collaboration platform that connects motivated learners with shared goals to learn, help and build together.

I enjoy making responsive web apps and I am open to freelance, remote projects as well as contributing to Open Source and Non Profits.

I blog about coding and my self-learning journeys on Medium. I am also a part of the Chingu Publication.

Interests

I have a wide range of interests. Some of them include mobile and Web Development, Knowledge Extraction, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Computational Linguistics as applied to Indian languages, Art, Indian Carnatic Music and Sanskrit.
